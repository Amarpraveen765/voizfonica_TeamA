Our History


VozFonica was founded in 2010, and since then it has
experienced significant growth in profit and share
(currently, the company has a market share of 8.9% in
India, up from 5.6% in 2017 and 3.9% in 2016).

A growth of 1.36% over the previous quarter was recorded
in the number of telephone subscribers in India from
1,194.58 million at the end of Mar-17 to 1,210.84 million
at the end of Jun-17. In comparison to last year's same
quarter,this represents 14.25% growth year-over-year.
